Getting Started with Statify

Here you can see how to invite the Discord Bot and setup you Statistics.







1. Inviting the Bot



Invite the Discord Bot to your Server by clicking here.
When you successfully invited the bot you will be redirected to a confirmation page.








2. Setting up Statistics



Enter the s!setup command and follow the steps shown on the screen.
If everything is complete a Statistic Message will be created in your selected Textchannel..








3. Customising the Bot



You can customise the Discord Bot by changing the language or the prefix.
Use s!prefix (new Prefix) or s!language (new Language)








4. Leaderboards & Users



Get the top 10 members with the most messages or voicetime with the s!leaderboard command.
Use s!leaderboard (messages/voicetime/textchannel/voicechannel)(guild/global).
You can also get the Statistics of a specific User by typing s!user (@User).








5. Charts



Know how active your community is by generating a own chart for your Discord Server.
Use s!chart (user/bots/invites/boosts/total_messages/total_voicetime/messages_today/voicetime_today)
















If you still need help with the Bot. Feel free to join our Discord Server and open a ticket in #ticket-support
